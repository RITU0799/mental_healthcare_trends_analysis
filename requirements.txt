# Requirements

## Libraries and Functions

1. Pandas – Data Handling & Manipulation

read_csv() – Load datasets from CSV files.
dropna() – Handle missing values efficiently.
groupby() – Aggregate data by specific categories.
merge() – Combine multiple datasets for analysis.


2. NumPy – Numerical Computing

array() – Work with numerical data structures.
mean() – Calculate averages.
std() – Compute standard deviation for variability analysis.


3. Matplotlib – Data Visualization (Static)

plot() – Generate line plots.
bar() – Create bar charts.
show() – Render and display visualizations.


4. Seaborn – Advanced & Aesthetic Visualizations

heatmap() – Display correlation or intensity maps.
barplot() – Visualize categorical comparisons.
lineplot() – Show trends over time.


5. Scikit-learn – Machine Learning & Modeling

train_test_split() – Divide data into training and testing sets.
LinearRegression() – Perform regression modeling.
metrics.mean_squared_error – Evaluate model accuracy.


6. Statsmodels – Statistical Analysis

OLS() – Conduct Ordinary Least Squares regression.
summary() – Generate detailed model summaries.
conf_int() – Calculate confidence intervals.
